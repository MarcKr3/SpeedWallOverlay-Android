Privacy Policy

SpeedWall Overlay — Effective date: January 30, 2026

SpeedWall Overlay ("the App") is a climbing wall measurement and overlay tool developed by mV. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered.
  • No data is sent to any server or third party.
  • The App does not use advertising or ad tracking.
  • The App does not access the internet.

Camera Usage

The App requires access to your device's camera for its core functionality: displaying a real-time overlay on a climbing wall for measurement and calibration purposes. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device and are never stored, uploaded, or shared. The camera feed exists only in memory while the App is active.

Screenshots

When you choose to take a screenshot using the in-app button, the image is saved locally to your device's photo gallery. These files remain entirely on your device and are never transmitted externally.

Sensors

The App may access your device's accelerometer sensor for the auto-level feature. This sensor data is used in real time on the device only and is not recorded or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the developer contact information listed on the Google Play Store.
